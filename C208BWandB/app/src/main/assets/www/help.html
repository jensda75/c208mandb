General

Yellow fields are editable. As the BEM and Basic Arm changes over time, those values have to be taken from the actual Mass And Balance sheet in the airplane, to be inserted in first row.

Passenger Details

  1. Enter total passenger mass in kg.
  2. Enter total number of passengers.
  3. Under Seating, enter number of passengers for each row.
  4. For removing or installing seats, tick or untick in appropriate row under single or double, and weights will be automatically added or subtracted accordingly.
  5. If more accurate loading is needed, tick for individual passenger mass, and enter mass in kg for each seat.

Cargo Details

  1. Enter mass in kg. for each zone or pod.

Special Load

  1. Tick/untick for each item onboard/not onboard.
  2. Enter actual mass.
  3. The mass will be added to appropriate cargo zone or cargo pod, and will show in the "Special" coloumn.
If you experience problems with values after an update, just hit the "Total Reset" button.
